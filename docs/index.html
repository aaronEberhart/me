About Me

I am a Research and Development Engineer at metaphacts where I contribute to the Graph-Massivizer project and assist in the development of knowledge graph and AI applications. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Kansas State University, a BS in Computer Science at Wright State University, and a BA in Philosophy at the University of Pittsburgh.

I am primarily interested in computation and logic, with a focus on description logics and rule-based reasoning. In my spare time, I also consider methods for augmenting logical reasoning with machine learning strategies and neural networks.
